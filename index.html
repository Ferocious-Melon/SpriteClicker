How to Play

Welcome to Soot Sprite Clicker! Collect as many sprites as you can (until you get bored) and unlock all your favourite Ghibli characters, through upgrades and achievements.

Earning Sprites

The Sprites

Click the soot sprites to collect more, the faster they spin the more Click Value you'll earn.

Upgrades

Buy characters to increase CPS. This is your "clicks per second". It represents the amount of sprites you earn passively every second.

You can also buy upgrades which enhance your click value based on the characters you own.

Achievements

The more clicks you get and the more sprites you collect, the more achievements you unlock. These are just for fun, but they do come with some pretty cool titles and icons to show off your progress.
